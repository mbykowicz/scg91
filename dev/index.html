Hello, my name is Michael Scigniejew based in Southampton, UK. I’m highly creative and enthusiastic freelancer with some years of experience. Design and acute eye for detail is my passion. I’m open minded which helps me to adapt design to meet changing clients needs. Able to manage projects from conception through to completion, consulting with clients, printers and other external suppliers to provide the highest quality solutions.
